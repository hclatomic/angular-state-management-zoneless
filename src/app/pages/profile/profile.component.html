Note that no validate button is needed here. Of course it would be better in order to control the inputs before they are injected into the app, but we proceed this way here to demonstrate the power of the services associated to the change detection loop.

Also note that leaving "street" and "city" empty, the billing process of your first order will ask you this information, that will be stored into your profile. Ordering a second time, the billing process will not propose you any more to complete this information, that you will be able however to modify from the present profile page.
